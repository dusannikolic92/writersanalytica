Strategy

Content Strategy

Developing a content strategy involves several key steps to ensure that the content aligns with your values, resonates with the target audience, and effectively communicates the brand's message. This includes understanding the language used by your target audience, planning the content that reflects your identity and your brand, and planning the strategies for promoting this content.

Competitor

Competitor Analysis

Competitor analysis consists of identification and evaluation of  linguistic practices your competitors use. This process includes identification of your competitors, the analysis of content used by your competitors to promote and market their services, as well as monitoring the feedback they receive from their customers.


NLP

Language models

Our language models use machine learning to conduct probability distributions over words, phrases, and even sentences.
Rather than focusing on grammar, the models concentrate on understanding syntactic and semantic structure used by your services and by the services in your field.
