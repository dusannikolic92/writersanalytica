Our Mission

We founded Writers Analytica with the mission to improve your business through modern analytics. We aim to provide all customers with research-based information on linguistic practices of most successful companies in their field and provide solutions.

Core Values

We achieve integrity by delivering reliable and research-based information to our clients. We focus on innovation, teamwork, social responsibility, and professionalism in our approaches.

Our CEO

Dr Dušan Nikolić has a Ph.D. in Linguistics received at the University of Calgary, in Canada. He is an expert in linguistic analysis and has many years of experience working in industry as data analyst.
